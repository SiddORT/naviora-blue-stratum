# Naviora by Blue Stratum

## Sprint 2.7 – Organizations, Organization Portal & User Management

Build the complete Organization Management and Organization Portal module.

This sprint transforms approved onboarding requests into active organizations with their own isolated portal.

Organizations must be able to:

* Login to their portal
* Manage users
* Manage candidates
* View subscription details
* Access entitled assessments
* Access entitled simulators
* View reports
* Manage organization settings

All organization data must be isolated.

Organizations must never see another organization's data.

Follow platform standards:

* FastAPI
* PostgreSQL
* SQLAlchemy 2.0
* Alembic
* Next.js 15
* TypeScript
* JWT Authentication
* Audit Logging
* Encryption Framework
* GDPR Compliance

Generate production-ready code.
